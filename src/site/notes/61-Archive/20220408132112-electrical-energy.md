# Electrical Energy

In electrical engineering, [Power](20220223192844-power.md) is the rate at which [Energy](20220223192810-energy.md) is produced, consumed, or dissipated.

$P=IV$

Using [Ohm's Law](20220408120857-ohm's-law.md),

$P=IV=I^{2}R=\frac{V^{2}}{R}$

## Deriving the Formula

The formula can be derived from the general [Power](20220223192844-power.md) formula.

$$
\begin{align*}
P&=\frac{\Delta E}{\Delta t}\\
&=\frac{\Delta E}{\Delta q} \frac{\Delta q}{\Delta t}\\
&=VI
\end{align*}
$$
