# Rogue Access Points

>[!word] Rogue Access Points
> An unauthorized wireless access point 
> - May be added by an employee or an attacker 
> - Not necessarily malicious 
> - A significant potential [[62-Encyclopedic/62.01-security-plus/20220602145709-backdoor|backdoor]]  
> 
> How to create
> - Very #easy to plug in a wireless AP 
> - enable wireless sharing in your OS
> 
> How to prevent 
> - Schedule a periodic survey and reviews of the wireless environment 
>     - Ensure that the only wireless AP are the ones I put there 
>     - Walk around your building/ campus 
>     - Use third-party tools/ Wi-Fi Pineapple 
> - Consider using Network Access Controls 
>     - Such as 802.1X
>     - You must authenticate, regardless of the connection type
